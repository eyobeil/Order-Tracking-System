Welcome to the Order Tracking System (OTR)

An order tracking system allows the customer to gain insight into where their order is at any given point in time. To create and manage the Customers and Admin, please click the appropriate menu item, located on the menu-bar above.

Customers need to follow their order from its initial placement right up until it arrives at their door. Order tracking system is able to follow and provide insight into the status of the order to make the order process user friendly
